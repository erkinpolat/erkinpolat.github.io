GANs (Generative Adversarial Network) are a recent way of generating data using neural networks. A GAN usually consists of a generator network that produces data and a discriminator network that tries to identify whether a given data is real or fake. These two networks compete with each other in a zero sum game and over time both get more competent. In the ideal scenario generator improves to the point where the discriminator can make correct predictions only half of the time and a convergence is achieved where the generated data is very convincing.
